Privacy Policy

Overview

Simplybreath is designed to help users relax, focus, and sleep through guided breathing. We value your privacy and keep the app completely free from data collection and tracking.

Data Collection

Simplybreath does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. The app does not track usage, behavior, or device information.

Permissions

The app does not request access to sensitive device permissions such as location, contacts, storage, or camera.

Third-Party Services

The app may provide an optional donation link powered by Razorpay. If you choose to make a donation, you will be redirected to Razorpay's secure platform.

All payment processing is handled by Razorpay. Gowri Studio does not collect, process, or store any payment details such as card information or bank data. Your interaction with Razorpay is governed by their privacy policy and terms.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, no user data is stored or transmitted by the app.

Children’s Privacy

Simplybreath does not knowingly collect any data from children.
